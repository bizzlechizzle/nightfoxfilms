## Week 2: Choose Platform & Start Building

### Decision: What Platform?

**Option 1: Squarespace** (Easiest, fastest)
- Pros: Beautiful templates, easy to use, no coding
- Cons: Less customization, monthly cost ($20-40/mo)
- Timeline: 2-3 weeks to build
- **Best for:** DIY build, fast launch

**Option 2: Webflow** (Middle ground)
- Pros: More customization, visual builder, CMS for archive
- Cons: Steeper learning curve, monthly cost ($15-35/mo)
- Timeline: 3-4 weeks to build
- **Best for:** Design-forward, scalable

**Option 3: WordPress** (Most flexible)
- Pros: Unlimited customization, plugins, cheaper hosting
- Cons: Requires more technical knowledge or developer
- Timeline: 4-6 weeks to build
- **Best for:** Long-term, full control

**Option 4: Custom Build** (Highest quality)
- Pros: Exactly what you want, unique, optimized
- Cons: Most expensive ($3,000-10,000), longest timeline
- Timeline: 6-8 weeks
- **Best for:** If budget allows, ultimate quality
