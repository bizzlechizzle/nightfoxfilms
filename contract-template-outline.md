Nightfox Films is NOT liable for any damages beyond the refund of payments.

## Section 5: Coverage Details & Responsibilities

### 5.1 Coverage Hours

**Package includes coverage for:**
- [X hours] of continuous coverage from [start time] to [end time], OR
- All-day coverage (no time limits) for Mixed Media packages

**Overtime:**
- Not available for fixed-hour packages
- Mixed Media packages have no overtime (all-day included)

### 5.2 Videographer Responsibilities

- Arrive on time and prepared with all necessary equipment

## Section 6: Film & Footage Ownership

### 6.1 Client Ownership

**Client owns:**
- All raw footage (Super 8 scans, digital files, VHS-style footage)
- All edited films delivered as part of the package
- The right to duplicate, share, and use footage for personal use

**Client does NOT own:**
- The right to resell or commercially license the footage
- Copyright to the edited films (Videographer retains copyright)

### 6.2 Videographer Usage Rights

**Nightfox Films retains the right to:**
- Use wedding footage for portfolio, website, social media, and marketing purposes
- Enter films into contests or submit to publications
- Share client testimonials and reviews

**If Client wishes to opt out of portfolio use:**
- Client must notify Videographer in writing
- Opting out may result in a 10% surcharge ($200-$600 depending on package)

### 6.3 Music Licensing

Nightfox Films will use licensed music or Client's requested songs (subject to licensing availability).

**Client is responsible for:**
- Ensuring any requested songs are legally available for use in wedding films
- Understanding that some songs may have copyright restrictions

If requested song is unavailable, Videographer will suggest alternatives.

## Section 7: Revisions & Edits

### 7.1 Included Revisions

Each package includes **1 round of revisions** for the final edited film.

**Revisions include:**
- Minor edits (trimming clips, adjusting music timing, color correction tweaks)
- Song changes (if licensing allows)
- Reordering of clips

**Revisions do NOT include:**
- Complete re-edits
- Adding footage not originally shot
- Changing the overall style or aesthetic

### 7.2 Additional Revisions

**Additional revision rounds:** $200 per round

**Major re-edits (complete overhaul):** $500+

Revisions must be requested within 30 days of receiving final film.

### 7.3 Revision Timeline

Revisions will be completed within 2-4 weeks of request, depending on scope and season.

## Section 8: Force Majeure (Acts of God)

### 8.1 Definition

**Force majeure includes:**
- Natural disasters (hurricanes, floods, earthquakes, blizzards)
- Pandemics or government-mandated event restrictions
- Severe illness or injury preventing Videographer from working
- Venue closure or cancellation
- Any event outside Videographer's reasonable control

### 8.2 If Force Majeure Occurs

**If the wedding is postponed or cancelled due to force majeure:**
- Deposit may be transferred to a new date within 24 months (subject to availability)
- No penalty or additional fees
- If no suitable new date is available, deposit is refunded in full

**If Videographer cannot provide coverage due to force majeure:**
- Videographer will attempt to provide a qualified replacement
- If replacement is not acceptable, Client receives full refund of all payments

## Section 9: Liability & Limitations

### 9.1 Equipment Failure & Backup

Nightfox Films brings backup equipment to all weddings.

**In the unlikely event of complete equipment failure:**
- Videographer will make every effort to resolve the issue immediately
- If coverage cannot be completed, Client will receive a partial or full refund proportional to missed coverage

Nightfox Films is NOT liable for damages beyond the refund of payments.

### 9.2 Film Processing & Lab Errors

**For Super 8 packages:**
- Film is processed by third-party labs (Cinelab, Pro8mm, etc.)
- Nightfox Films is NOT responsible for lab errors, lost film, or processing delays outside our control
- Nightfox Films will work with labs to resolve any issues, but cannot guarantee outcomes

**Super 8 film has inherent risks:**
- Grain, light leaks, and imperfections are part of the medium (not defects)
- Client acknowledges and accepts these characteristics when booking Super 8

### 9.3 Lost or Corrupted Footage

Nightfox Films takes all precautions to protect footage (multiple backups, cloud storage, redundant drives).

**In the event of lost or corrupted footage:**
- Videographer will attempt recovery using professional services
- If footage is unrecoverable, Client will receive a refund proportional to lost footage
- Videographer is NOT liable for damages, lost memories, or emotional distress beyond refund of payments

**Maximum liability:** Total amount paid by Client

### 9.4 Venue Restrictions

Some venues have restrictions on videography (no flash, restricted areas, etc.).

**Client is responsible for:**
- Informing Videographer of all venue restrictions
- Obtaining necessary permissions from venue

**If venue restrictions prevent coverage:**
- Videographer is NOT responsible for missed footage
- No refunds will be issued for footage missed due to venue restrictions

### 9.5 Missed Moments

Nightfox Films will make every effort to capture all key moments outlined in the client questionnaire.

**However, Videographer is NOT liable if:**
- A moment occurs outside of contracted coverage hours
- Client did not communicate the moment in advance
- Moment occurs in a restricted area or during a restricted time
- Technical issues (dead microphone, obstructed view, etc.) prevent capture

**Best effort is guaranteed; perfection is not.**

## Why This Contract Works

### Protects You (Nightfox Films):
1. **Non-refundable deposit** ensures you don't lose income from cancellations
2. **Force majeure clause** protects you from COVID-style disruptions
3. **Liability limitations** cap your risk at refund amount (not unlimited damages)
4. **Film processing disclaimers** protect you from lab errors outside your control
5. **Portfolio usage rights** allow you to use work for marketing

### Protects Client:
1. **Clear deliverables** define exactly what they're getting
2. **Refund terms** outline when they get money back
3. **Revision policy** ensures they can request changes
4. **Backup equipment clause** shows you're prepared
5. **Force majeure protections** allow date transfers for COVID, weather, etc.

### Reduces Friction:
- Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)
- Clear timelines (when they'll receive film)
- Defined responsibilities (who does what)
- Revision policy (sets expectations)

**Result:** Professional, legally sound contract that builds trust and protects both parties.
