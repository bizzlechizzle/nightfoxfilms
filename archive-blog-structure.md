# Archive/Blog Structure & Guidelines
**Purpose:** SEO content machine + social proof + portfolio showcase

---

## Archive URL Structure

### Main Archive Page
**URL:** `/archive` or `/films`

**Purpose:** Showcase all wedding films with filters

**Layout:**
- Grid of wedding film thumbnails (4-col desktop, 2-col tablet, 1-col mobile)
- Filters: Medium (Super 8, Dad Cam, Modern Digital, Mixed Media), Location, Year
- Each card: Thumbnail, couple names, location, medium, date

---

## Individual Wedding Film Post Structure

### URL Format
`/archive/[couple-first-names]-[location]-[medium]-wedding`

**Examples:**
- `/archive/sarah-mike-finger-lakes-super-8-wedding`
- `/archive/emma-chris-rochester-dad-cam-wedding`
- `/archive/jake-lauren-adks-mixed-media-wedding`

**Why this structure?**
- **SEO gold:** Every URL contains location + medium keywords
- **Readable:** Easy for humans and search engines
- **Shareable:** Couples can share their specific film page

## Content Calendar & Publishing Strategy

### Posting Frequency
**Target:** 1-2 archive posts per month

**Why?**
- Keeps site fresh for Google
- Builds portfolio showcase
- Each post = new SEO opportunity
- Gives couples a shareable link to their film

### Timing
**Best time to publish:** When couples receive their final films (2-4 months after wedding)

**Delayed publishing:** Publish archive posts year-round, not just wedding season (keeps site active in off-season)

## Archive Post Checklist

Before publishing each archive post, ensure:

- [ ] Video is embedded and plays properly
- [ ] Couple names are correct (get permission to use full names or first names only)
- [ ] Location/venue name is accurate and spelled correctly
- [ ] Medium tags are applied (Super 8, Dad Cam, etc.)
- [ ] Meta title and description are optimized with location + medium keywords
- [ ] At least 3-5 image stills from the film are included
- [ ] Image alt text includes location and medium keywords
- [ ] Internal links to relevant service pages are included
- [ ] Vendor credits are included (great for backlinks)
- [ ] Testimonial quote from couple is included (if available)
- [ ] Schema markup is added
- [ ] Mobile responsiveness is tested
- [ ] Social sharing is tested (couples will share this!)

## Vendor Collaboration for Backlinks

### Strategy
**Tag vendors in every archive post** (venue, photographer, florist, planner)

**Why?**
- Vendors will link back to your site from their portfolios
- Backlinks = SEO boost
- Creates referral network

**How:**
- Include vendor credits section in every post
- Tag vendors in Instagram/social shares of archive posts
- Email vendors when post goes live with shareable link

### Example Vendor Credits Section
```
## Vendor Love

**Venue:** [Bristol Harbour](https://bristolharbour.com)
**Photography:** [Jane Smith Photography](https://janesmithphoto.com)
**Florist:** [Petal & Stem](https://petalandstem.com)
**Planning:** [Finger Lakes Weddings](https://fingerlakesweddings.com)
```
