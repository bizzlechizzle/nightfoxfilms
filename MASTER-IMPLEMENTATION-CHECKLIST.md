## üö® COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Launching without testing** - Test every form, link, and page before going live
2. **Not responding fast** - 50% of couples book first responder; aim for <1 hour response time
3. **Forgetting mobile** - 60% of traffic is mobile; test everything on your phone
4. **Generic copy** - Use YOUR voice, not template copy (customize everything!)
5. **No clear CTA** - Every page should have obvious next step (Book consultation, View pricing, Contact us)
6. **Hiding pricing** - Transparency builds trust; show pricing clearly
7. **Ignoring SEO** - Add alt text, meta descriptions, and optimize from day one
8. **Not collecting testimonials** - Ask EVERY client for review; social proof = bookings
9. **Inconsistent Instagram** - Post 3-5x per week minimum; consistency > perfection
10. **Not tracking metrics** - Check Google Analytics weekly; know what's working
