## Behind the Scenes

### Headline (H2)
Filming This Day

### Body Copy
Filming a wedding on Super 8 is all about patience and intentionality. Each roll of film shoots for about 2.5 minutes, so we have to be strategic about what moments to capture.

For Sarah and Mike's wedding, we used:
- **Roll 1 (Indoor):** Getting ready moments, first look, ceremony details
- **Roll 2 (Outdoor):** Ceremony on the lawn, couple portraits by the lake
- **Roll 3 (Extra):** Golden hour vineyard shots, first dance, reception dancing

After the wedding, the film was sent to a specialized lab in Los Angeles for hand processing and frame-by-frame scanning. Six weeks later, we received the digitized footage and began editing their signature 6-minute film.

The result? Timeless, nostalgic, and exactly what Sarah and Mike envisioned.
