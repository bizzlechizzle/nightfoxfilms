# About Page Wireframe
**URL:** /about
**Purpose:** Humanize your brand, build trust, tell your story

---

## Why the About Page Matters

**Couples don't book businesses. They book people.**

The About page is where you build trust, show personality, and prove you're the right fit for their wedding.

**Key questions couples have:**
- Who will actually be filming my wedding?
- Why do they care about film vs digital?
- Can I trust them with my once-in-a-lifetime day?
- Do they "get" me and my vibe?

## Copy Guidelines

### Tone:
- Warm, friendly, authentic
- Not overly formal or corporate
- Show personality (you're real people, not a brand)
- Vulnerable (share why you do this work, not just what you do)

### Structure:
- Short paragraphs (3-5 sentences max)
- Conversational (write like you talk)
- Story-driven (hook â†’ journey â†’ mission)

## Why This Page Converts

**Psychology:**
