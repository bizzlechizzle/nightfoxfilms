**High-Intent Super 8 Buyer:**
1. Lands on hero → Sees "$2,800" immediately (price confidence)
2. Scrolls to pricing section → Reviews base package + add-ons
3. Reads FAQ → Concerns addressed
4. Clicks "Book a Free Consultation"

**Curious Researcher:**
1. Lands on hero → Watches film loop
2. Reads "What Makes Super 8 Special?" → Educated on the medium
3. Watches film samples → Emotional connection
4. Reviews pricing → Considers budget
5. Reads testimonials → Social proof
6. Either books or compares to Mixed Media (cross-sell)

**Mobile Visitor:**
1. Hero loads (optimized)
2. Immediately sees pricing (builds trust)
3. Watches one sample film (embedded player)
4. Taps "Book Now" sticky button
5. Contact form or calendar opens
