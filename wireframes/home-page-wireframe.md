## User Flow

**High-Intent Visitor (Knows They Want Super 8):**
1. Lands on hero → Immediately sees "Your Wedding on Real Film"
2. Scrolls to Service Selector → Clicks "Super 8"
3. Routed to Super 8 dedicated page

**Browsing Visitor (Exploring Options):**
1. Lands on hero → Watches looping film clips
2. Scrolls through Service Selector → Compares all options
3. Reads "Why Film Matters" section → Educated on value
4. Views Recent Work → Social proof
5. Clicks CTA "Book a Consultation"

**Mobile Visitor:**
1. Hero loads quickly (optimized video)
2. Service cards stack vertically
3. "Book Now" sticky button floats bottom of screen
4. One-tap call or email contact

## Technical Requirements

### Performance:
- **Page load:** < 2 seconds
- **Video background:** Lazy-loaded, mobile-optimized
- **Images:** WebP format, responsive srcset

### SEO:
- **Meta title:** "Nightfox Films | Super 8 & Analog Wedding Videography in Rochester, NY"
- **Meta description:** "The only Super 8, VHS, and analog film wedding videographers in Upstate New York. Serving Rochester, Buffalo, Finger Lakes, and the Adirondacks."
- **Schema markup:** LocalBusiness, VideoObject
- **Alt text:** Descriptive for all images/videos

### Accessibility:
- **ARIA labels:** For navigation and CTAs
- **Keyboard navigation:** Full site accessible via keyboard
- **Color contrast:** WCAG AA compliant

## Conversion Optimization Notes

1. **Pricing Visibility:** Show starting prices on home page (builds trust)
2. **Multiple CTAs:** "Book Now," "Learn More," "View Work" at every section
3. **Clear Service Differentiation:** Each service card is visually distinct
4. **Social Proof Early:** Testimonials and recent work above the fold (mobile)
5. **Location Transparency:** Travel fees clearly stated (reduces friction)
6. **Emotional Hook:** "Your Wedding on Real Film" speaks to nostalgia immediately

---

## A/B Testing Ideas

- **Hero CTA:** "Explore Our Work" vs "See Our Films"
- **Pricing:** "Starting at $2,800" vs "From $2,800" vs pricing hidden
- **Service Cards:** Grid layout vs carousel slider
- **Video Background:** Looping clips vs single atmospheric clip vs static image

---

## Analytics & Tracking

**Key Metrics:**
- Time on page
- Scroll depth (% reaching Service Selector section)
- Click-through rate on service cards
- "Book Now" CTA clicks
- Exit rate (where do people leave?)

**Heatmap:** Monitor which service gets most attention

**Goal Funnels:**
1. Home → Service Page → Contact
2. Home → Archive → Contact
3. Home → Direct Booking CTA
