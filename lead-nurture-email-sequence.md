**Trigger:** When someone inquires but doesn't book within 48 hours

**Expected Conversion Rate:** 20-30% of nurture sequence recipients book

## END OF SEQUENCE

**What Happens Next?**

### If They Book:
- Remove from nurture sequence
- Add to "Booked Clients" email list
- Send booked confirmation email (see template)
- Begin client onboarding process

### If They Don't Respond:
- Move to "Cold Leads" list
- Send seasonal promotion email in 3-6 months
- Keep them on general newsletter (if you have one)

### If They Book Someone Else:
- Send "Thanks but no thanks" response (see template)
- Remove from all email lists
- Ask for feedback (what made them choose someone else?)

## Nurture Sequence Settings

### Email Timing:
- Email 1: Day 0 (48 hours after inquiry with no response)
- Email 2: Day 2 (2 days after Email 1)
- Email 3: Day 5 (3 days after Email 2)
- Email 4: Day 10 (5 days after Email 3)
- Email 5: Day 15 (5 days after Email 4)

### Delivery Time:
- Send between 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM (highest open rates)
- Avoid weekends (couples are busy)
- Tuesday-Thursday = best days

### Unsubscribe Option:
- Include at bottom of every email (legally required)
- "Not interested? [Unsubscribe here]"

---

## Success Metrics

### Benchmarks:
- **Open Rate:** 40-60% (wedding emails perform well)
- **Click Rate:** 10-20% (on emails with links to films)
- **Response Rate:** 15-25% (couples replying with questions)
- **Conversion Rate:** 20-30% (booking after nurture sequence)

### What to Track:
- Which email gets the most responses? (optimize that one)
- Which email has highest unsubscribe rate? (tone it down)
- How many people book after Email 3? (social proof works!)
- How many people respond to Email 5 urgency? (scarcity works!)

---

## Customization Tips

### Personalization:
- Use [Name], [Date], [Venue] merge tags
- Reference their specific package interest (Super 8, Dad Cam, etc.)
- Mention details from consultation call (if applicable)

### A/B Testing Ideas:
- **Subject lines:** Question vs statement vs urgency
- **Email length:** Short vs long-form
- **CTA:** "Book now" vs "Let's chat" vs "Reply with questions"
- **Social proof:** Testimonial quote vs full case study

### Seasonal Adjustments:
- **Peak season (May-Oct):** More urgency ("dates filling fast")
- **Off-season (Nov-Apr):** Offer discount in Email 4 or 5
- **Holiday season:** Add "lock in 2025 pricing" messaging

---

## Why This Sequence Works

**Email 1:** Removes friction (did you get my email?)
**Email 2:** Educates (why film matters)
**Email 3:** Proves (social proof from real couple)
**Email 4:** Addresses objections (removes anxiety)
**Email 5:** Creates urgency (FOMO)

**Result:** 20-30% of people who enter this sequence will book. Without it, you're losing 1 out of 5 potential bookings.

## Platform Recommendations

### Mailchimp (Easiest):
- Free up to 500 contacts
- Drag-and-drop email builder
- Basic automation

### ConvertKit (Best for Creators):
- $15/month (up to 300 subscribers)
- Advanced automation
- Tag-based segmentation

### HoneyBook (All-in-One):
- $16/month
- Includes CRM, contracts, invoices, email automation
- Built for wedding vendors

**Recommendation:** Start with Mailchimp (free), upgrade to ConvertKit or HoneyBook when you have 20+ inquiries per month.

---

## Manual Alternative (If No Budget for Email Platform)

**Use Gmail + Reminders:**

1. Create a Google Doc with all 5 email templates
2. When someone inquires, set calendar reminders:
   - Day 2: Send Email 1
   - Day 4: Send Email 2
   - Day 7: Send Email 3
   - Day 12: Send Email 4
   - Day 17: Send Email 5
3. Copy/paste and personalize each email manually

**Pros:** Free
**Cons:** Manual work, easy to forget, not scalable

**Bottom line:** Invest in email automation as soon as you can afford it. It pays for itself with one booking.
